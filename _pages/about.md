Hello. I am Xinming Li, born in 2002 in Yantai, Shandong Province, China. I am currently a Ph.D. student at the College of Design and Engineering (CDE), National University of Singapore (NUS), where I began my doctoral studies in 2024 under the supervision of Prof. Xiao Gong. My research focuses on high-performance memory design.

Prior to joining NUS, I received my Bachelor of Engineering degree from Shandong University in 2024, where I was supervised by Prof. Yuan Li. During my undergraduate studies, my research focused on first-principles calculations of high-performance tunneling field-effect transistors (TFETs).
